.:. how to print this card .:.

download the svg. print at 100% — not fit to page. margins as small as possible (0 if you can).

best: set a custom paper size to 125x312 (portrait), same as the file, then print 100%. everything fits on one sheet.

if your printer won’t take that length, use a4 anyway. you align top-left of the card to top-left of the paper. the bottom may run off the sheet. don’t shrink to fit.

black dots are holes. cut the outer rectangle with scissors, then punch the dots. the scale check line should measure 45 mm on paper. if not, the printer scaled it...

put the card in the machine with row 1 at the top.
